About Me

Data enthusiast who enjoys analyzing, visualizing and storytelling using various data for intelligent business decisions.

After graduating from Stony Brook Univeristy with B.S. in Clinical Laboratory Science, she worked at various major hospitals in NYC, including Mt.Sinai hospital and NYU Langone, for over 5 years.

While working with a clinical data, she found the emerging field of data science and its real impact in healthcare industry and decided to pursue a Masters degree in Information Systems, concentrating on data analytics, at New Jersey Institute of Technology.

Equipped with Data Science Certificate from NYCDSA, She is now working as a data analyst at ProHealth analyzing financial and clinical data. She is proficient in SQL (SQLServer), Tableau, Excel and Python while has an experience in R/RStudio and Spark.

Aside from working on her data science projects, she enjoys traveling, volunteering, singing and learning photography. If you want to discuss any cool data science or photography projects, feel free to get in touch with Bee using the LinkedIn link above the page. Thank you!
